Auto Strategy Discovery
Bot evaluates all 6 strategies on every trade. Fitness score = regime fit × weight + historical win-rate × weight + recent performance × weight. Weights shift automatically from regime-heavy → stats-heavy as data accumulates. Best strategy is auto-selected per trade.
